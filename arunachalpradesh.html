Facts About Arunachal Pradesh

  1. Earlier named after North East Frontier Agency

    Initially called as NEFA,on january 20,1972 that it was renamed as Arunachal Pradesh.

  2. Largest of the seven sisters

    Arunachal Pradesh is the largest North Eastern State with an area of 83,743 SQ KM.

  3. Lots and lots of O2

    it has a forest cover of over 82%.

  4. International neighbours

    It has an International border of 1630 KM with China,Myanmar and Bhutan.

  5. Hub of tribes and diverse cultures

    The State is inhabitated by 26 major tribes and 100 sub-tribes.

  6. You gotta have a permit to roam in your home state!

    Even the Domestic tourists need a special permit,inner line permits to enter Arunachal Pradesh.

  7. Massive monastery of Twang

    The 400 years old TWANG monastery is the largest monastery in INDIA and the second largest in the World.

  8. Let’s go back in time!

    Neolithic tools that are around 11,000 years old have been found in Arunachal Pradesh.

  9. The river bridge that connects two states

    India's longest river bridge is Dhola-Sadiya,which connects Arunachal Pradesh with Assam.

  10. Let’s converse in English, y’all!

    English is the only official language in Arunachal Pradesh.

  11. Diverse tourist circuits of Arunachal

    Arunachal Pradesh has 12 tourist circuits which vary from one another in terms of Ethnic Culture ,Topography and Vegetation.

  12. You can’t fly here!

    Arunachal Pradesh has no functional Airport,airports of Assam are the nearst Airways.

  13. Rain God is too generous here

    Ir receives an average rainfall of above 3000MM annually.

  14. Not so crowded!

    It has the thiniest population density in the Country with just 13 persons per sq km.

  15. Come here for a flower-ful experience

    The state is Home to over 500 species of Orchids.
