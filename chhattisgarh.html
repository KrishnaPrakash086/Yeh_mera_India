Facts About Chhattisgarh

  1. Home to famous Kosa Silk

    Kosa silk, a variety of Tussar silk. It is famous world over for its texture and hue and is produced in korba and champa in Chhattisgarh from cocoons on arjun, sal and saja tree.

  2. Many, many tribes of Chattisgarh

    Chhattisgarh is one of the staes with majority of tribal population. Schedule caste and schedule tribes taken together form 50% of the population.

  3. Saviors on motorcycles

    Motorcycle abmulance is being used to ave lives of people who live in remote area of chhattisgarh where ambulance cannot reach. So far, it has saved the lives of more than 200 pregnant women.

  4. he Niagara Falls of India

    Chitrkot Waterfalls is located near Jagdalpur in Bastar district. The name of its river is Indravati. Due to its large span during monsoon season it is also called as Niagra Falls of India.

  5. The state of steel

    Chhattisgarh accounts for15% of the total steel produced in india.

  6. The film industry of Chattisgarh

    Chhattisgarh's film industry is called as Chhollywood. mainly, locally funded films are produced here.

  7. The exotic temple of Bhoramdeo

    Bhoramdeo temple located in kabirdham district was built around 11th century by nagwanshi kings. Due to its erotic sculptures it is also called the Khajurho of Chhattisgarh.

  8. The traditional anecdotal music of Chattisgarh

    Pandavani is a folk singing style of Chhattisgarh. It involves narration of tales from the ancient india epic of mahabharata.

  9. The road that Lord Ram walked

    ram van gaman marg, the present day route from where lord Ram is said to have passed during the period when he was exiled, extends from Ratnapur to Ramoram.

  10. The Grand Festival of Chattisgarh

    It is one of the biggest festivals here. the people worship a local deity Maa Danteswai and the festival lasts for 75 days.

  11. The ancient brick temple of Chattisgarh

    Sirpur's 77th century Laxman temple is one of the oldest brick temples of india. Unlike its name, It is actually dedicated to lord Vishnu.

  12. The shaky wonder called Jal Jali

    Jal-Jali, It is a unique place as anyone who jumps here can create eathquake.

  13. The birthplace of Saint Vallabhacharya

    Champaran, it is the birth place of saint Vallabhacharya, who founded the Vallabh sect.

  14. The reputed music university of Chattisgarh

    India's first university dedicated to music and dance, The indra kala sangeet vishwavidyalaya is located at Khairagarh in Chhattisgarh.

  15. The interesting temple of Talagram

    Devrani-Jethani Temple Excavation, is famous for the 6th rudra shiva idol, which is an artistic assembly of different animals and insects and has many human faces on its body.
