Facts About Haryana

  1. A Battle hub

    SEVERAL HISTORIC BATTLES SUCH AS THE BATTLES OF PANIPAT AND KURUKSHETRA OF MAHABHARATA WERE FOUGHT HERE.

  2. 100% electricity

    HARYANA IS THE FIRST STATE IN INDIA TO HAVE 100 PERCENT RURAL ELECTRIFICATION.

  3. Rice for the world!

    HARYANA IS THE LARGEST EXPORTER OF BASMATI RICE IN INIDA.

  4. Twin Capital

    SINCE THE STATE WAS LATER CARVED OUT FROM PUNJAB, HARYANA SHARES ITS CAPITAL CITY WITH PUNJAB-CHANDIGARH.

  5. Hosts ancient civilizations

    THE HARAPPA CIVILIZATION STARTED FROM RAKHIGARHI AND THERE ARE TRACES OF IT EXTENDING TO HISAR DISTRICT.

  6. The curious case of Saraswati River

    THE RIVER BANKS OF SARASWATI, WHICH IS THE PRESENT DAY HARYANA, TRACES TO THE REGION WHERE THE VEDAS WERE WRITTEN.

  7. Land of champions

    IN THE 2010 COMMONWEALTH GAMES AT DELHI,22 OUT OF 38 GOLD MEDALS THAT INDIA WON CAME FROM HARYANA.

  8. The history of Gurugram

    IT IS BELIEVED THAT GURU DRONACHARYA GAVE SPIRITUAL INSTRUCTION TO THE PANDAVAS AND THE KAURAVAS AT THIS PLACE.

  9. Beautiful birds sanctuary

    THIS 145-HECTARE NATIONAL PARK THAT PLAYS HOST TO OVER 250 RESIDENT AND MIGRATORY BIRD SPECIES, IS LOCATED IN GURUGRAM.

  10. The legendary Sun Pool

    IT IS NAMED AFTER THE 10TH-CENTURY SUN POOL THAT WAS BUILT HERE BY RAJA SURAJPAL, LEADER OF THE SUN-WORSHIPPING TOMAR CLAN.

  11. The state of live entertainment

    IT IS INDIAS FIRST LIVE ENTERTAINMENT , THEATRE AND LEISURE DESTINATION.

  12. The origin of universe

    ACCORDING TO LEGENDS, LORD BRAHMA CREATED THE UNIVERSE FROM THE BRAHMA SAROVAR OF KURUKSHETRA AFTER PERFORMING HUGE YAJNA.

  13. Ancient history behind Panipat

    THE CITY OF PANIPAT FINDS MENTION IN MAHABHARATA AS ONE OF THE FIVE CITIES FOUNDED BY THE FIVE PANDAVA BROTHERS.

  14. The Spiky Garden

    NATIONAL CACTUS AND SUCCULENT BOTANICAL GARDEN AND RESEARCH CENTRE IS ASIA'S BIGGEST CACTI GARDEN AND HAS MORE THAN 3500 SPECIES OF CACTI.

  15. Mango Bonanza

    AN ANNUAL EVENT, THE MANGO IS ORGANISED IN THE MONTH OF JUNE OR JULY AT MUGHALIN PINJORE.STARTED IN 1992 AND HAS MANGOES FROM ALL OVER THE COUNTRY.
