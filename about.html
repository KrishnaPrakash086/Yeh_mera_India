Incredible INDIA

India is a country located in South Asia. It is the second-most populous country in the world, with a population of over 1.3 billion people. The official language is Hindi, but there are also many other languages spoken throughout the country. The capital of India is New Delhi, and the largest city is Mumbai (formerly known as Bombay). India has a diverse culture, with influences from various religions including Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, and Sikhism. The country has a mixed economy, with agriculture, manufacturing, and services being the major sectors. India is also known for its rich history and ancient civilization, with landmarks such as the Taj Mahal and the ancient city of Harappa.
